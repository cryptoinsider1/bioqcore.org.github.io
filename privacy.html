Privacy Policy

Privacy by design and data minimization.

This website is designed to collect the minimum information required for public contact and partner inquiry workflows.

Version: 1.0.0-rc · Effective date: 2026-06-13 · Review status: public draft for legal review

Summary

BioQCore.org is an informational and research-oriented website. Public forms, if used, must not collect protected health information, medical records, scans, genomic data, DICOM files or patient-identifiable information.

Data we may collect

  • Name, organization, role, email, country and short inquiry text if voluntarily provided.
  • Basic security and access logs necessary for site operation and abuse prevention.

Purposes

  • Responding to non-emergency public inquiries.
  • Evaluating partnership, research, grant or compliance discussions.
  • Protecting the website from abuse and security incidents.

Boundaries

No patient data, medical records, genomic data, scans, DICOM files, emergency requests or confidential files may be submitted through public forms or public email.

User rights and contact

For privacy-related questions, contact contact@bioqcore.org. This policy will be expanded as operational systems, processors and compliant channels are formally activated.

No sale of personal data

BioQCore.org does not sell personal data. Advertising pixels are not used in this MVP release.
